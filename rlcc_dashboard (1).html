Market Cycle Context
What drove each phase
2015–2019 · Steady Growth

Desert luxury market appreciation of ~4–5%/yr. Low interest rates, stable demand from seasonal buyers. $/sqft rose from ~$215 to ~$258.

2020–2022 · COVID Surge

Remote work exodus to desert drove demand sharply. $/sqft doubled from ~$265 (Q1 2020) to $465+ (Q1 2022). Sales volume spiked, then compressed as rates rose.

2023–2025 · Re-Anchoring

Price gains moderated to ~5–7%/yr. Volume stabilized at ~50–62 sales/yr. Days on market extended to 35–91. Still a seller's market, but buyers gaining footing.

1031 Exchange — Key Considerations
RLCC-specific timing and inventory analysis
⏱️

45-Day Identification Window

You must identify replacement properties within 45 days of closing your relinquished property. With only ~7 active listings in your target models at any time, tour before your clock starts.

📅

180-Day Close Deadline

You must close the replacement property within 180 days. RLCC averages 35–91 days on market, so timing is workable — but leave margin for negotiation and escrow.

📦

Low Inventory Environment

The community closes ~50–62 homes/year total. Your 3 target models combined see ~22 sales/year — roughly 1–2 per month. Limiting to one model raises execution risk.

✅

Keeping 3 Models Open — Smart Strategy

Encanto I is your preference but Encanto II and Terraza give you 3× the opportunity. All three track closely in appreciation and are recognized as strong comps by lenders and appraisers.

🔄

Consider a Reverse 1031

If timing is tight, you can close on the RLCC property first, then sell the relinquished property within 180 days. Requires an exchange accommodation titleholder (EAT) — consult your QI.

💰 Practical negotiating room right now

At a $1.5M Encanto I ask, ~3.2% average discount = ~$48K. Homes 46–90 DOM are where you have the most leverage — that's your sweet spot. Over 90 DOM can yield 5–6%, but always ask why it hasn't sold.

🗓️ Best 1031 timing window: May–June

Season is winding down, motivated sellers remain, competition from snowbird buyers drops sharply. DOM extends to 45–70 days, giving you 3–4% room. Better than Jan–Mar (peak, 1–2% room) or Aug–Sep (thin choices).

⚠️ Bedroom count: verify casita inclusion

Encanto II and Terraza II are MLS-listed as either 3 BR or 4 BR depending on whether the detached casita is counted. This affects comp searches — always ask the listing agent which count is used and verify against the floor plan.

📦 26% of listings don't sell — use this

A meaningful share of RLCC listings are withdrawn or expire. Re-listed homes often come back at lower prices and with motivated sellers. Ask your agent to flag any home that was previously listed — this is a hidden source of deal flow.
